WHAT TO DO

  1. Cease All Payments and Communications: Immediately stop sending any more money and block all contact with the scammer.
  2. Contact Your Financial Institution: Reach out to your bank or financial service provider as soon as possible to report the scam. Request them to halt any transactions or freeze your accounts if necessary.
  3. Protect Personal Information: If your personal details are compromised, inform your financial institutions, change passwords, and be vigilant for suspicious activities.
  4. Report the Scam: File a report with Scamwatch or the police, and consider reaching out to the Australian Financial Complaints Authority if you're not satisfied with your bank's response.
